Our Services

  1. Home
  2. Services

Environmental Management

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA), Basic Assessment (BA), Permits, licences and Authorization: WULA, WMLA, Biodiversity permits. Environmental planning, monitoring, and reporting. Environmental compliance audits, Health and Safety Risk Assessment, Environmental Site Inspection, Feasibility site assessment and risk evaluation, Rehabilitation plans, Climate change, mitigation strategies and adaptation.

Geological & Mining services

Geotechnical (geological hazard assessment, Geotechnical investigation, Terrain stability analysis). Mining (prospecting rights, mining permits/right applications and renewals). Mineral exploration (exploration project design, desktop study, geological and structural mapping, mineral exploration, geophysical) and geochemical surveys (data collection, analysis, and interpretation), drilling and core logging, prospectively analysis and target generation. Mining hydrogeology.

Geospatial & Drone (UAV) services

Stockpile volumetric calculations, Land rehabilitation monitoring, Topographic surveys, Deformation monitoring, Tailings dam inspections and monitoring, Landfill management, Topographical surveys, geophysical exploration surveys, Environmental sensitivity analysis and mapping, Spatial data and open data solutions, Site selection analysis, management, and planning, Potential mineral mapping, Time series analysis and environmental compliance

Precision Agricultural Services

Soil survey and mapping, land use and capability mapping. Soil sampling, testing and analysis. Yield mapping reports to build a record of your farm. Crop surveys, analysis, platform development and monitoring. Farm planning and landscape design. Crop health assessment and monitoring. Soil Impact Assessment and Management Plans. Soil monitoring and rehabilitation. Agricultural feasibility studies.

Water Resource Management

Groundwater and surface water investigation and modelling. Catchment management strategies and planning. Hydrological modelling. River health and mitigation strategies. Harmful algae blooms detection, Aquatic plant investigation. Wetland planning, assessment, and management. Flood hydrology and flood lines modelling. Water conservation and demand management plan. Water use license authority (WULA). Rehabilitation, wetland maintenance and management plans, hydropedology assessments, Aquatic assessment and monitoring, ecological risk assessments, alien invasive assessments and management plans). Storm Water Management Plans.

Waste Management

Solid waste collection (Residential waste, commercial and industrial). Development of transfer station models. Development of sanitary landfill models. Development of waste recovery models (recycling, reduce and reuse). Development of Integrated Waste Management Plan (IWMP). Waste Management Master Plan. Review By-laws. Awareness and education. Landfill site identification (suitability) and design. Landfill auditing.
